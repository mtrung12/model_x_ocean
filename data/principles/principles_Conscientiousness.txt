1. Language Structure: Structured and coherent writing vs informal and disorganized writing.  
2. Vocabulary Use: Formal and precise vocabulary vs casual and vague vocabulary.  
3. Emotional Tone: Reflective and introspective tone vs anxious and frustrated tone.  
4. Planning Approach: Proactive planning and time management vs impulsive and reactive behavior.  
5. Self-Reflection: High levels of introspection and analysis vs low levels of self-reflection and self-doubt.  
6. Concerns Focus: Achievement-oriented concerns about future success vs avoidance of responsibilities and procrastination.  
7. Pronoun Usage: Occasional collective references indicating social support vs frequent first-person focus indicating individualism.
